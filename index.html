Ralph Bakshi

THE EARLY YEARS

        Ralph Bakshi began October 1938 in Haifa, Palestine. In 1939 his family brought him and his sister to New York, escaping the threat of war. He 
        grew up in Brooklyn, attending the High School of Industrial Arts, now called the High School of Art & Design. After graduating in 1956 with an
        award in cartooning, Bakshi went to work at Terrytoons Animation Studio in New Rochelle as a cel polisher, soon graduating to cel painting.
